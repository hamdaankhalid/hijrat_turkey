Turkish Citizenship By Investment (TCBI)

Program Routes

The Regulation Regarding Application of Turkish Citizenship Code was amended on 18th September 2018. Turkish immigration law allows foreign persons to obtain Turkish Citizenships together with their family members (spouse and children below 18 years) without any obligation to reside in Turkey, provided they make investments in Turkey as follows:

  • Route (A)

    Buy any property worth $250,000: This is the simplest route to Turkish Citizenship. It stipulates that a minimum amount of property investment shall be made for $250.000 USD (or the equal value in Turkish Lira). This property can be residential, commercial or office space. In approximately four months from the date of title deed registry, you can have Turkish Passport in your hand without any need to surrender current passport or any obligation to stay or work in Turkey. The property bought could be self-occupied or leased out with only condition that it may not be sold for next three years. After three years, the property can be sold without any implication on your rights as Turkish Citizen. Bingo! Turkey welcomes you with open heart.

  • Route (B)

    Deposit $500,000 in Turkish Bank: This route requires a deposit of $500.000 USD (or the equal value in Turkish Lira) for 3 years in one of the Turkish Banks.

  • Route (C)

    $500,000 Fixed Capital Investment: This route requires that a fixed capital investment of $500.000 USD (or the equal value in Turkish Liras). This investment shall be confirmed by the Turkish Ministry of Industry and Technology.

  • Route (D)

    $500,000 Government Bond: This route for Turkish Citizenship needs purchase of Turkish Government Bonds and Bills of $500.000 USD on the condition to hold them for at least 3 years and needs to be confirmed by the Ministry of Treasury and Finance of Turkey.

  • Route (E)

    $500,000 of Real Estate Trust: This route envisagesTurkish Citizenship acquisition by making an investment into a Turkish real estate investment trust, or alternatively with purchasing the shares of a Turkish Venture Capital Investment Fund with a minimum amount of $500.000 USD.
