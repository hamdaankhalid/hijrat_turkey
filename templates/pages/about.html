Aim

We at hijrat-turkey.com aim to build a small India in Turkey by helping Indians to acquire Citizenship or Residency through investment. To create a community support system to encourage seamless living for Indian origin persons from across the globe in Turkey.

Our Dream

We are a team of professionals who are engaged in promoting Turkey as alternative destination for Indians who are looking to explore countries like Canada, US, Australia, UK, UAE and Germany. Turkey is a land of opportunity and has opened its heart to welcome citizens across the world and Indians have an opportunity which is historical in nature. We bring Indians together in this mission and connect them to certified service provider like Property Consultants, Immigration Lawyers and Bankers. We help Indians strike best deals and secure their investments with proper guidance and advise. “Little India” in Istanbul with few thousand flourishing Indian families basking in Bosphorus Sea beaches is our dream.
